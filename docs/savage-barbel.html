SAVAGE BARBELL

Savage Barbell is a growing functional fitness apparel company. The company designs, manufactures and distributes a popular range of high-quality fitness apparel out of California, USA and will soon open distribution hubs in Dubai, South Africa and Europe. The product range has a proven track record and is endorsed by world-class athletes.

Savage Barbell has several million dollars of profitable revenue and is already a well-known brand in the functional fitness sector. The company has an exciting roadmap for global expansion and is also planning expand its range into other sports.
